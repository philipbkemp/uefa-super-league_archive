UEFA Super League

The UEFA Super League is something I made to see how is the best league team in Europe. Starting with the first recognised season of football (the 1888/89 English Football League) and working up to today, teams are ranked in a league system that combines the top flight of every nation under UEFA. Some notes about the league:

Rules and Notes
  • To make the leagues consistent, wins are always worth 3 points, and a draw 1 point.
  • To avoid favouring teams in larger leagues (where more points are available due to more matches being played), teams are ranked by Points Per Game.
  • In the case of a tie, teams will then be ranked by Goals Per Game.
  • The league (when big enough) will be split into Divisions, with each Division having a maximum of 20 teams.
  • Once large enough, 3 teams will be relegated/promoted between the Divisions - either the lowest 3, or 3 teams who are relegated from the national top flight, or a combination thereof. If more than 3 teams are relegated (due to relegation from their national top flight), the same number of teams will be promoted to cover them.
  • Teams relegated from their national top flight will be removed from the League. If they are promoted again, they will rejoin the League in the first available space.
  • All new or rejoining Teams will be sorted by the usual tie break rules and then placed into the available Divisions in that order.
  • Any Team who has points deducted by their national association will have the same points deducation added in this League.
  • Playoff matches to decide if a Team is relegated, or other matches outside the normal League season, are not counted, unless otherwise noted.
